Palm Coast Physical Therapy offers personalized care delivered exclusively by two doctors of physical therapy. As a privately owned, therapist-led clinic, we focus on lasting results – not just symptom relief, through compassionate, individualized treatment. With advanced training in manual therapy, vestibular rehab, and neuromuscular reeducation, we're committed to clinical excellence using a compassionate and professional approach.
